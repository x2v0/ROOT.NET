To build release version just click on the "pbuild.bat" file,
for debug virsion pass "debug" at the command line, i.e. "pbuild.bat debug"
To cleanup - run "pclean.bat"

After successive build executables will be located
in the .\bin directory, for example .\bin\hsimple\hsimple.exe
